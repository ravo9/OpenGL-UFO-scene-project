To control UFO vehicle, choose camera 4 and press any arrow key!

WSAD, ZX - to control the UFO vehicle

E - to drop the bomb

T - to go out of the world (Magic Ball view)
Y - to come back from the Magic Ball view
I, U - to control the Magic Ball view

H - to activate fireworks

M - to activate the earthquake

B - to activate the Blur Motion
N - to deactivate the Blur Motion
